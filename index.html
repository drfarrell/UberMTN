UberMTN

UberPool for Colorado's mountain-lifestyle

Abstract

Colorado's mountain passes are packed with traffic every weekend. Uber has an excellent business opportunity of bringing a modified version of UberPool to colorado. Doing so will not only strengthen the ride-sharing company's reputation, but help to alleviate Colorado's mountain-traffic problems.


UberMTN can provide affordable and reliable transportation to and from the Rockies while easing traffic on major corridors
